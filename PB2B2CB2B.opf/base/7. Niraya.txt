[7. Nirayaparivarta]

jātyandhakoṭiniyutānyavināyakānāṃ mārge akovidu kuto nagarapraveśe |vina prajña pañca imi pāramitā
acakṣuḥ avināyakā na prabhavanti spṛśetu bodhim || 7,1 ||

yatrāntarasmi bhavate pragṛhīta prajñā tatu labdhacakṣu bhavatī imu nāmadheyam |yatha citrakarmapariniṣṭhita
cakṣuhīno na ca tāva puṇyu labhate akaritva cakṣuḥ || 7,2 ||

yada dharma saṃskṛta asaṃskṛta kṛṣṇaśuklo aṇumātru no labhati prajña vibhāvamānaḥ |yada prajñapāramita
gacchati saṃkhya loke ākāśa yatra na pratiṣṭhitu kiṃci tatra || 7,3 ||

saci manyate ahu carāmi jināna prajñāṃ mociṣya sattvaniyutāṃ bahurogaspṛṣṭān |ayu sattvasaṃjñaparikalpaku
bodhisattvo na ca eṣa prajñavarapāramitāya caryā || 7,4 ||

yo bodhisattva varapāramiteti cīrṇo paricārikā ya na ca kāṅkṣati paṇḍitehi |saha śrutva tasya
puna bheṣyati śāstṛsaṃjñā so vā laghū anubudhiṣyati bodhi śāntām || 7,5 ||

satkṛtya buddhaniyutāṃ paricārikāyāṃ na ca prajñapāramita śraddadhitā jinānām |śrutvā ca so
imu kṣipiṣyati so ’lpabuddhiḥ sa kṣipitva yāsyati avīcim atrāṇabhūto || 7,6 ||

tasmā hu śraddadhata eva jināna mātāṃ yadi icchathā spṛśitu uttamabuddhajñānam |so vāṇijo yatha
vrajitvana ratnadvīpaṃ mūlātu chedana karitva puna āgameyā || 7,7 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ nirayaparivarto nāma saptamaḥ ||
