[19. Gaṅgadevābhaginīparivarta]

tailasya varti jvalitā prathame nipāte na ca dagdha varti asatā na vinā ya dagdhā |na hi arci
paścimanipāta sa varti dagdhā asatā pi paścima na dahyati dīpavarti || 19,1 ||

prathameva citta spṛśatī na ca agrabodhim asatā na tasya spṛśatā puna śakya bhonti |na ca citta
paścima śivām anuprāpuṇāti asatā na tasya puna prāpaṇanāya śakyam || 19,2 ||

bījātu stamba phala puṣpa samāgamanti so vāniruddha asato na hi tasya vṛkṣo |emeva citta prathamaṃ
tu nidāna bodheḥ so vā niruddha asato na hi tasya bodhiḥ || 19,3 ||

bījaṃ pratītya ca bhaved yavaśālikādes tattatphalaṃ na ca tad asti na cāpi nāsti |utpattito
bhavati bodhir iyaṃ jinānāṃ bhāvasvabhāvavigatā bhavatīha māyā || 19,4 ||

udakabindu kumbha paripūryati stokastokaṃ prathame nipāti anupūrva sa paścimena |emeva citta
prathamaṃ varabodhihetur anupūrva śuklaguṇapūrṇa bhavanti buddhāḥ || 19,5 ||

śūnyānimittapraṇidhiṃ caramāṇu dharmā na ca nirvṛtiṃ spṛśati no ca nimittacārī |yatha nāviko
kuśala gacchati ārapāram ubhayānti asthitu na tiṣṭhati arṇavesmin || 19,6 ||

evaṃ carantu na ca manyati bodhisattvo ahu vyākṛto daśabalehi spṛśeya bodhim |na ca trāsu bodhi
bhavate na ihāsti kiṃcid evaṃ carantu caratī sugatāna prajñām || 19,7 ||

kāntāramārgi durabhikṣi savyādhi lokāṃ paśyitva nāsti bhaya uttari saṃnahante |aparāntakoṭi
sada yukta prajānamāno aṇumātra kheda manaso na upādiyāti || 19,8 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ gaṅgadevābhaginīparivarto nāmaikānnaviṃśatimaḥ ||
