[5. Puṇyaparyāyaparivarta]

saci rūpa saṃjña api vedana cetanāyāṃ cittaṃ anitya pariṇāmayi bodhisattvo |pra(ti)varṇikāya
carate aprajānamāno na hi dharma paṇḍita vināśa karoti jātu || 5,1 ||

yasmin na rūpa api vedana cāpi saṃjñā vijñāna naiva na pi cetanayopalabdhiḥ |anupādu śūnya na
ya jānati sarvadharmān eṣā sa prajñavarapāramitāya caryā || 5,2 ||

yāvanti gaṅganadivālikatulyakṣetre tāvanti sattva arahanti vineya kaścit |yaś caiva prajña ima
pāramitā likhitvā parasattvi pustaku dadeya viśiṣṭapuṇyaḥ || 5,3 ||

kiṃ kāraṇaṃ ta iha śikṣita vādiśreṣṭhā gamayanti dharma nikhilāniha śūnyatāyām |yāṃ śrutva śrāvaka
spṛśanti vimukti śīghraṃ pratyekabodhi spṛśayanti ca buddhabodhim || 5,4 ||

asato ’ṅkurasya drumasaṃbhavu nāsti loke kuta śākhapatraphalapuṣpaupādu tatra |vina bodhicitta
jinasaṃbhavu nāsti loke kuta śakrabrahmaphala śrāvakaprādubhāvaḥ || 5,5 ||

ādityamaṇḍalu yadā prabhajāla muñcī karmakriyāsu tada sattva parākramanti |tatha bodhicitta
sada lokavidusya jñāto jñānena sarvaguṇadharma samāgamanti || 5,6 ||

yatha nopatapta asato bhujagādhipasya kuta nadyaprasravu bhavediha jambudvīpe |asatā nadīya
phalapuṣpa na saṃbhaveyuḥ na ca sāgarāṇa ratanā bhavi naikarūpāḥ || 5,7 ||

tatha bodhicitta asatīha tathāgatasya kuta jñānaprasravu bhavediha sarvaloke |jñānasya co asati
nāsti guṇāna vṛddhiḥ na ca bodhi sāgarasamā na ca buddhadharmāḥ || 5,8 ||

yāvanti loki kvaci jotikaprāṇabhūtā obhāsanārtha prabha osarayanti sarve |varasūryamaṇḍalaviniḥsṛta
ekaraśmī na kalā pi jyotikagaṇe siya sarva-ābhāḥ || 5,9 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ puṇyaparyāyaparivarto nāma pañcamaḥ ||
