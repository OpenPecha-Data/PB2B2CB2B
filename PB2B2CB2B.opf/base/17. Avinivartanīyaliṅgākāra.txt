[17. Avinivartanīyaliṅgākāraparivarta]

sthaviro subhūti paripṛcchati lokanāthaṃ araṇāya liṅga bhaṇahī guṇasāgarāṇām |avivartiyā yatha
bhavanti mahānubhāvā tāṃ vyākuruṣva jinaguṇāna pradeśamātram || 17,1 ||

nānātvasaṃjñavigatā gira yuktabhāṇī na ca anya te śramaṇa brāhmaṇa āśrayanti |triyapāyavarjita
vidū sadakāli bhonti daśabhiś ca te kuśalakarmapathebhi yuktā || 17,2 ||

dharmaṃ nirāmiṣu jagasyanuśāsayanti ekāntadharmaniyatāḥ sada snigdhavākyāḥ |sthiticaṃkramaṃ
śayaniṣadya susaṃprajānā yugamātraprekṣiṇa vrajanty abhrāntacintā || 17,3 ||

śuciśaucaambaradharā trivivekaśuddhā na ca lābhakāma vṛṣabhā sada dharmakāmāḥ |mārasyatītaviṣayā
aparapraṇeyā catudhyānadhyāyi na ca niśrita tatra dhyāne || 17,4 ||

na ca kīrtikāma na ca krodhaparītacittā gṛhibhūta nitya anadhyoṣita sarva vastuṃ |na ca jīvikāviṣayabhoga
gaveṣayanti abhicāramantra na ca istriprayogamantrāḥ || 17,5 ||

na ca ādiśanti puruṣaiḥ striya icchakarmāṃ pravivikta prajñavarapāramitābhiyuktāḥ |kalahāvivādavigatā
dṛḍhamaitracittā sarvajñakāma sada śāsani nimnacittāḥ || 17,6 ||

pratyantamlecchajanavarjita-antadeśāḥ svakabhūmi kāṅkṣavigatāḥ sada merukalpāḥ |dharmārtha jīvita
tyajanti prayuktayogā avivartiyāna imi liṅga prajānitavyā || 17,7 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyām avinivartanīyaliṅgākāraparivarto nāma saptadaśamaḥ ||
