[28. Avakīrṇakusumaparivarta]

yāvanti śikṣa paridīpita nāyakena sarveṣa śikṣa ayu agru niruttarā ca |yaḥ sarvaśikṣavidu icchati
pāra gantum imu prajñapāramita śikṣati buddhaśikṣām || 28,1 ||

agraṃ nidhāna ayu uttamadharmakośa buddhāna gotrajananaṃ sukhasaukhyagañjo |atikrāntanāgata
daśaddiśi lokanāthā itu te prasūta na ca kṣīyati dharmadhātuḥ || 28,2 ||

yāvanti vṛkṣa phalapuṣpavanaspatī yā sarve ca medinisamudgata prādubhūtāḥ |na ca medinī kṣayamupaiti
na cāpi vṛddhiṃ na ca khidyatī na parihāyati nirvikalpā || 28,3 ||

yāvanta buddhasama śrāvakapratyayāś ca marutaś ca sarvajagatī sukhasaukhyadharmāḥ |sarve ti
prajñavarapāramitāprasūtā na ca kṣīyate na ca vivardhati jātu prajñā || 28,4 ||

yāvanta sattva mṛdumadhyamutkṛṣṭa loke sarve avidyaprabhavā sugatena uktāḥ |sāmagripratyayu
pravartati duḥkhayantro na ca yantra kṣīyati avidya na cāpi vṛddhiḥ || 28,5 ||

yāvanti jñāna nayadvāra uyāyamūlāḥ sarve ti prajñavarapāramitāprasūtāḥ |sāmagripratyaya pravartati
jñānayantro na ca prajñapāramita vardhati hīyate vā || 28,6 ||

yo tu pratītyasamutpādu anudbhavāye imu prajña akṣayata budhyati bodhisattvo |so sūrya abhrapaṭale
yatha muktaraśmī vidhamitvavidyapaṭalaṃ bhavate svayaṃbhūḥ || 28,7 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyām avakīrṇakusumaparivarto nāmāṣṭāviṃśatimaḥ ||
