[4. Guṇaparikīrtanaparivarta]

śakro jinena paripṛcchitu praśnamāhu saci gaṅgavālikasamā siya buddhakṣetrāḥ |jinadhātu sarvi
paripūrita cūḍibaddhā imam eva prajñavarapāramitāhu gṛhṇe || 4,1 ||

kiṃ kāraṇaṃ na mi śarīri agauravatvaṃ api tū khu prajñaparibhāvita pūjayanti |yatha rājaniśrita
naro labhi sarvi pūjāṃ tatha prajñapāramitaniśrita buddhadhātuḥ || 4,2 ||

maṇiratna sarvi guṇayukta anarghaprāpto yasmiṃ karaṇḍaki bhave sa namasyanīyaḥ |tasyāpi uddhṛta
spṛhanti karaṇḍakasmiṃ tasyaiva te guṇa mahāratanasya bhonti || 4,3 ||

emeva prajñavarapāramitāguṇāni yannirvṛte ’pi jinadhātu labhanti pūjām |tasmā hu tān jinaguṇā(n)
parighettukāmo so prajñapāramita gṛhṇatu eṣa mokṣo || 4,4 ||

pūrvaṃgamā bhavatu dānu dadantu prajñā śīle ca kṣānti tatha vīrya tathaiva dhyāne |parigrāhikā
kuśaladharma-avipraṇāśe ekā ca sā api nidarśayi sarvadharmān || 4. 5 ||

yatha jambudvīpi bahuvṛkṣasahasrakoṭī nānāprakāra vividhāśca anekarūpāḥ |na vi chāyanānatu bhaveta
viśeṣatāpi anyatra chāyagatasaṃkhya prabhāṣamāṇā || 4,6 ||

emeva pañca imi pāramitā jinānāṃ prajñāya pāramita nāmatayā bhavanti |sarvajñatāya pariṇāmayamāṇa
sarve ṣaḍapīha ekanayam archati bodhināmā || 4,7 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ guṇaparikīrtanaparivarto nāma caturthaḥ ||
