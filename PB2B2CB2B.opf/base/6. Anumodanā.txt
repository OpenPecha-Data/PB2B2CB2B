[6. Anumodanāparivarta]

yāvanti śrāvakagaṇāḥ prasavanti puṇyaṃ dānaṃ ca śīlamapi bhāvanasaṃprayuktam |sa hi bodhisattva
anumodana ekacitte na ca sarvaśrāvakagaṇe siya puṇyaskandho || 6,1 ||

ye buddhakoṭiniyutā purimā vyatītā ye vā anantabahukṣetrasahasrakoṭayaḥ |tiṣṭhanti ye ’pi parinirvṛta
lokanāthā deśanti dharmaratanaṃ dukhasaṃkṣayāya || 6,2 ||

prathamaṃ upādu varabodhayi cittupādo yāvat su dharmakṣayakālu vināyakānām |ekasmi tatra ciya
teṣa jināna puṇyaṃ saha yukta pāramita ye ’pi ca buddhadharmāḥ || 6,3 ||

yaś caiva buddhatanayāna(ca) śrāvakāṇāṃ śaikṣa aśaikṣa kuśalāsrava nāsravāśca |paripiṇḍayitva
anumodayi bodhisattvo sarvaṃ ca nāmayi jagārthanidāna bodhi || 6,4 ||

pariṇāmayantu yadi vartati cittasaṃjñā tatha bodhisattvapariṇāmana sattvasaṃjñā |saṃjñāya dṛṣṭisthitu
citta trisaṅgayukto pariṇāmitaṃ na bhavatī upalabhyamānam || 6,5 ||

saci eva jānati nirudhyati kṣīṇadharmā taccaita kṣīṇa pariṇāmayiṣyanti yatra |na ca dharma dharmi
pariṇāmayate kadācit pariṇāmitaṃ bhavati eva prajānamāne || 6,6 ||

saci so nimitta kurute na ca mānayāti atha ānimitta pariṇāmita bhonti bodhau |viṣasṛṣṭa bhojanu
yathaiva kriyāpraṇīto tatha śukladharmaupalambha jinena ukto || 6,7 ||

tasmā hu nāmapariṇāmana śikṣitavyā yatha te jinā kuśala eva prajānayanti |yajjātiyo ’yaṃ prabhavo
yadalakṣaṇaṃ ca anumodamī tatha tathā pariṇāmayāmi || 6,8 ||

evaṃ ca puṇya pariṇāmayamāna bodhau na ca so hi buddha kṣipate jina uktavādī |yāvanti loki upalambhikabodhisattvā
abhibhonti sarvi pariṇāmayamāna śūro || 6,9 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyām anumodanāparivarto nāma ṣaṣṭhamaḥ ||
