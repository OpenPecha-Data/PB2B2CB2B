[27. Sāraparivarta]

evaṃ carantu vidu nā pṛthudevasaṃghāḥ kṛtaañjalīpuṭa praṇamya namasyayanti |buddhā pi yāvata
daśaddiśi lokadhātau guṇavarṇamālaparikīrtana kurvayanti || 27,1 ||

yāvanti gaṅganadivālisame hi kṣetre sattvā ta sarvi parikalpa bhaveyu mārāḥ |ekaika roma puna
tāntaka nirmiṇeyyā sarve na śakya karaṇe vidu antarāyam || 27,2 ||

catukāraṇehi balavāṃ vidu bodhisattvo bhavate dugharṣu catumāraasaṃprakampyo |śūnyāvihāri bhavate
na ca sattvatyāgī yathavādi sattvakaruṇānugatāvasthānaḥ || 27,3 ||

yo bodhisattva adhimucyati bhāṣyamāṇām ima prajñapāramita māta tathāgatānām |pratipattiyā ca
abhiyujyati āśayena sarvajñatāya abhiprasthitu veditavyo || 27,4 ||

na ca dharmadhātutathatāya upaiti sthānaṃ bhavatī athānasthita so laghu antarīkṣe |vidyādharo
va abhilambhu vanābhiprāyā khagu kālahīna druma mantrabalādhiṣṭhāno || 27,5 ||

evaṃ carantu vidu paṇḍitu bodhisattvo na ca budhyakaṃ labhati nāpi ca buddhadharmān |na ca deśikaṃ
na pi ca paśyaka dharmatāyāṃ śāntaiṣiṇām ayu vihāra guṇe ratānām || 27,6 ||

yāvanta śrāvakavihāra sapratyayānāṃ śāntā samādhipraśame sukhasaṃprayuktā |arhanvimokṣa sthapayitva
tathāgatānāṃ sarveṣu agra ayu vihāru niruttaraś ca || 27,7 ||

ākāśi pakṣi viharāti na co patāti dakamadhyi matsya viharāti na co marāti |emeva dhyānabalapāragu
bodhisattvo śūnyāvihāri na ca nirvṛti prāpuṇāti || 27,8 ||

yo sarvasattvaguṇa-agratu gantukāmo agraṃ spṛśeya paramādbhuta buddhajñānam |agraṃ dadeya vara
uttamadharmadānam imu agru sevatu vihāru hitaṃkarāṇām || 27,9 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ sāraparivarto nāma saptaviṃśatimaḥ ||
