[23. Śakraparivarta]

udayāti sūryu vigatāśca marīcimālā vidhamitva sarva tamasākulamandhakāram |avibhonti sarva krimijotikaprāṇabhūtāṃ
sarvāṃśca tārakagaṇānapi candra-ābhām || 23,1 ||

emeva prajñavarapāramitāṃ caranto vidhamitva dṛṣṭigahanaṃ vidu bodhisattvo |abhibhonti sarvajagatī
rahapratyayāṃśca śūnyānimittacarito pṛthu bodhisattvo || 23,2 ||

yatha rājaputra dhanadāyaku arthakāmo sarveṣu śreṣṭha bhavate abhigāminīye |sa hyeṣa etarahi
sattva pramocayāti prāgeva rājyasthitu bheṣyati paṭṭadhārī || 23,3 ||

emeva prajñacarito vidu bodhisattvo amṛtasya dāyaku priyo marumānuṣāṇām |ayu eṣa eṣati hi sattvasukhābhiyukto
prāgeva yāva sthitu bheṣyati dharmarājo || 23,4 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ śakraparivarto nāma trayoviṃśatitamaḥ ||
