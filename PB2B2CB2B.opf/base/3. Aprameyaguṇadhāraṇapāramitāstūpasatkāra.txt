[3. aprameyaguṇadhāraṇapāramitāstūpasatkāraparivarta]

ya imāṃ grahīṣyati paryāpuṇatī sa nityaṃ prajñāya pāramita yatra caranti nāthāḥ |viṣa vahni
śastra udakaṃ na kramāti tasyo otāru māru na ca vindati mārapakṣo || 3,1 ||

parinirvṛtasya sugatasya kareyya stūpāṃ pūjeya saptaratanāmayu kaścid eva |tehi prapūrṇa siya
kṣetrasahasrakoṭyo yatha gaṅgavālikasamaiḥ sugatasya stūpaiḥ || 3,2 ||

yāvanta sattva puna tāntaka kṣetrakoṭyo te sarvi pūjana kareyur anantakalpān |divyehi puṣpavaragandhavilepanehi
kalpāṃs triyadhvaparikalpa tato ’pi bhūyaḥ || 3,3 ||

yaś co imāṃ sugatamāta likhitva puste yata utpatī daśabalāna vināyakānām |dhāreyi satkarayi
puṣpavilepanehi kala puṇya bhonti na sa stūpi karitva pūjām || 3,4 ||

mahavidya prajña ayu pāramitā janānāṃ dukhadharmaśokaśamanī pṛthusattvadhātoḥ |ye ’tīta ye ’pi
ca daśaddiśa lokanāthā ima vidya śikṣita anuttaravaidyarājāḥ || 3,5 ||

ye vā caranti cariyāṃ hitasānukampām iha vidyaśikṣita vidu spṛśiṣyanti bodhim |ye saukhya saṃskṛta
asaṃskṛta ye ca saukhyā sarve ca saukhya prasutā itu veditavyāḥ || 3,6 ||

bījāḥ prakīrṇa pṛthivīsthita saṃbhavanti sāmagri labdhva viruhanti anekarupāḥ |yāvanti bodhiguṇa
pāramitāś ca pañca prajñāya pāramita te viruhanti sarve || 3,7 ||

yenaiva rāja vrajate sa ha cakravartī tenaiva sapta ratanā balakāya sarve |yenaiva prajña iya
pāramitā jinānāṃ tenaiva sarvaguṇadharma samāgamanti || 3,8 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyām aprameyaguṇadhāraṇapāramitāstūpasatkāraparivarto nāma tṛtīyaḥ
||
