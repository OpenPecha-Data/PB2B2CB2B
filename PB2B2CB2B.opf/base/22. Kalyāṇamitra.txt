[22. Kalyāṇamitraparivarta]

tasmā hu mānu nihanitvana paṇḍitena guruāśayena varabodhi gaveṣamāṇaḥ |vaidyo va ātura svarogacikitsanārthaṃ
kalyāṇamitra bhajitavya atandritena || 22,1 ||

buddhā ya bodhivaraprasthita bodhisattvā kalyāṇamitra imi pāramitā nidiṣṭāḥ |te cānuśāsaka iyaṃ
pratipattibhūmī duvi kāraṇena anubudhyati buddhabodhim || 22,2 ||

atikrāntanāgata jinā sthita ye diśāsu sarveṣu mārgu ayu pāramitā ananyo |obhāsa ulka varabodhayi
prasthitānām āloka śāstri imi pāramitā pradiṣṭāḥ || 22,3 ||

yatha prajñapāramita śūnyata lakṣaṇena tathalakṣaṇā ya imi jānati sarvadharmān |śūnyānalakṣaṇa
prajānayamāna dharmān evaṃ carantu caratī sugatāna prajñām || 22,4 ||

āhārakāma parikalpayamāna sattvāḥ saṃsāri yuktamanasaḥ sada saṃsmaranti |ahu mahya dharma ubhi
eti abhūta śūnyā ākāśagaṇṭhi ayu ātmana baddha bāle || 22,5 ||

yatha śaṅkitena viṣasaṃjñata abhyupaiti no cāsya koṣṭhagatu so viṣu pātyate ca |emeva bālupagato
ahu mahya eṣo ahasaṃjñi jāyi mriyate ca sadā abhūto || 22,6 ||

yatha udgraho tatha prakāśitu saṃkileśo vyodāna ukta ahu mahya anopalabdhi |na hi atra kaści
yo kliśyati śudhyate vā prajñāya pāramita budhyati bodhisattve || 22,7 ||

yāvanta sattva nikhile iha jambudvipe te sarvi bodhivaracitta upādayitvā |dānaṃ daditva bahuvarṣasahasrakoṭīḥ
sarvaṃ ca nāmayi jagārthanidāna bodhau || 22,8 ||

yaś caiva prajñavarapāramitābhiyukto divasaṃ pi antamaśa ekanuvartayeyā |kalapuṇya so na bhavatī
iha dānaskandho tadatandritena sada osaritavya prajñā || 22,9 ||

caramāṇu prajñavarapāramitāya yogī mahatīṃ janeti karuṇāṃ na ca sattvasaṃjñā |tada bhonti sarvajagatī
vidu dakṣiṇīyā satataṃ amoghu paribhuñjati rāṣṭrapiṇḍam || 22,10 ||

cirabuddhadevamanujān triapāyi sattvān parimocituṃ ya iha icchati bodhisattvo |pṛthumārgu tīru
upadarśayi sattvadhātau prajñāya pāramita yukta divā ca rātrau || 22,11 ||

puruṣo ya agru ratanasya alabdhapūrvo aparasmi kāli puna labdhva bhaveya tuṣṭo |sa ha labdhva
nāśayi puno ’pi pramādabhūto nāśitva duḥkhi satataṃ ratanābhikāṅkṣī || 22,12 ||

emeva bodhivaraprasthita ratnatulyo prajñāya pāramita yogu na riñcitavyo |ratanaṃ va labdhva
gṛhamāṇu abhinnasattvo anubuddhayati tvarito śivamabhyupaiti || 22,13 ||

bhagavatyāṃ ratnaguṇasaṃcayagāthāyāṃ kalyāṇamitraparivarto nāma dvāviṃśatitamaḥ ||
